About Me

Who am I?

I am Nidhi Gusai, a Data Analyst with an MSc in Agricultural Analytics from DAIICT Gandhinagar (CPI 8.6) and a B.Sc. in Horticulture from Anand Agricultural University. Currently working as a Data Analyst Executive at Omni Tech Engineering Ltd., Rajkot.

I specialise in building end-to-end data pipelines, interactive dashboards, and machine learning models. My work spans from forecasting groundnut prices with LSTM and GRU models to detecting tomato leaf diseases using deep learning and deploying AI APIs on AWS.

I love finding the story hidden inside data — and telling it clearly through visualisations, dashboards, and well-crafted models.
